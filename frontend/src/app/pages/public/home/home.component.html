How It Works

1

Log your expenses – Simply click "Add Expense", choose a category, set the amount and date. All your entries are saved instantly, allowing you to view and edit them anytime.

2

Auto-categorization & budgeting – TrackMate automatically groups your expenses and highlights when you're nearing your monthly limits, keeping your spending in check effortlessly.

3

Analyze via dashboard – Our dashboard visualizes your spending patterns with colorful charts, expense breakdowns, and trends, helping you make smarter financial decisions.

4

Set goals & get alerts – Create savings goals or monthly budgets, receive reminder alerts, and track your progress directly from your personal dashboard.
